About Us

Building tools for creators

At Piyu ImgExtract, we believe that gathering visual inspiration shouldn't be a chore. Whether you're a designer looking for mood board assets, a developer auditing a site, or a content creator collecting resources, our tool streamlines the process into a single click.

We started this project with a simple mission: to build the fastest, most reliable, and aesthetically pleasing image extraction tool on the web. No clutter, no ads, just pure utility wrapped in a beautiful interface.

Our Mission

To empower creatives by automating the tedious parts of their workflow, allowing them to focus on what primarily matters: Creating.
